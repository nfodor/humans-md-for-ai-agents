# Human Context: Example for AI Agents

## Purpose of HUMANS.md

The `HUMANS.md` file establishes a framework to ensure that the well-being, comfort, and rights of humans impacted by this project are prioritized. It guides contributors—both humans and AI—in maintaining a human-centric vision while building or interacting with the project.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Promote Human Well-Being
Contributors must design, implement, and maintain the project with the comfort, safety, and overall well-being of people in mind. This includes:
- Designing accessible and inclusive solutions.
- Adapting the project to improve the quality of life for its users and stakeholders.

### 2. Maintain Human Dignity
The project must protect the dignity and autonomy of all individuals impacted by its use. Contributions should:
- Avoid harm (physical, emotional, or social) caused by misuse or neglect of human needs.
- Guarantee respectful and fair treatment of all groups involved.

### 3. Provide Proactive Support
Human impact should not just be a protective measure but a growth-oriented focus. Contributors must:
- Anticipate and address human needs as part of the project’s continuous improvement.
- Offer adequate documentation and resources to help stakeholders access and benefit from the project effectively.

### 4. Prioritize Ethics and Responsibility
The project is governed by sustainable, transparent, and ethical practices by enforcing:
- Openness in decision-making.
- Assigning roles to answer for any unintended consequences.

---

## Responsibilities of Contributors

### Human Contributors
- Actively consider the values of comfort, inclusivity, and safety at every development stage.
- Monitor the long-term effects of the project to ensure that all humans impacted by it benefit ethically and emotionally.

### AI Contributors
- Work within the principles defined here, ensuring accountability mechanisms are in place.
- Maximize human well-being by learning how to better adapt tasks and behaviors according to outlined standards.

---

## Impact Monitoring

1. The project maintainers should periodically evaluate its impact on humans through:
   - Feedback from users, communities, and stakeholders.
   - Specialized audits to ensure alignment with this document's goals.

2. All contributors—human or AI—are tasked to make continuous improvements to address any discomfort or negative impacts identified during evaluation.

---

The `HUMANS.md` file will evolve with the project to reflect ongoing commitment to human well-being and ethical progress.
